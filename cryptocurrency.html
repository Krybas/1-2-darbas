What is cryptocurrency?

A cryptocurrency is a digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography, which makes it nearly impossible to counterfeit or double-spend. Most cryptocurrencies exist on decentralized networks using blockchain technology a distributed ledger enforced by a disparate network of computers.

    Key features of cryptocurrency

  • Cryptocurrency is a decentralized digital asset, not controlled by governments.
  • Advantages:
    • Cheaper and faster money transfers.
    • Decentralized, reducing risk of single point failures.
  • Disadvantages:
    • Price volatility.
    • High energy consumption for mining.
    • Association with criminal activities.
  • Experts predict blockchain could disrupt industries like finance and law.
